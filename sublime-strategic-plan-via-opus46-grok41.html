Executive Summary

Sublime Laser, Inc. is a niche player in precision laser machining for medical device components, specializing in femtosecond and fiber laser processing of tubular materials like nitinol and stainless steel. With ISO 13485 certification, fast turnaround (days vs. weeks), and services including R&D prototyping, design, part manufacturing, and "rent-a-laser," the company serves vascular device innovators in neurovascular, cardiovascular, and peripheral vascular segments.

Market Opportunity

The global medical laser market is projected to grow from $5.2B in 2024 to $8.5B by 2030 (CAGR 8.5%), driven by minimally invasive devices, aging populations, and R&D acceleration. Adjacent markets like aerospace microstructures and biotech offer expansion potential.

Competitive Landscape

Key competitors include Resonetics (scale leader, broad capabilities), Potomac Photonics (micromachining focus), Oxford Lasers (femtosecond expertise), and Nanotech Precision (tubing specialists). Sublime differentiates via speed, small-team agility, and Utah's med device hub location.

3-Year Vision

Become the go-to partner for ultrafast prototyping in medtech and expand into high-growth adjacencies (aerospace, biotech), achieving 3x revenue growth ($3M to $9M assumed baseline) through innovation in AI-driven design, hybrid manufacturing, and global partnerships.

Key Goals

  • Revenue: $4.5M (2025), $6.5M (2026), $9M (2027)
  • EBITDA Margin: 25% by 2027
  • New Customers: 50% from expansions
  • Innovation: Launch 2 new service lines
