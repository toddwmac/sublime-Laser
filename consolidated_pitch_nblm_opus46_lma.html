Company Overview

Boutique Precision, Enterprise Impact

Sublime Laser, Inc. is a boutique contract manufacturer specializing in the ultra-precision machining of small-diameter metal tubes and sensitive materials.

The Femtosecond Advantage

The femtosecond laser is the gold standard for machining sensitive alloys like Nitinol. Because pulses are ultra-short (10⁻¹⁵ seconds), material vaporizes before heat can diffuse into the surrounding area. This preserves the unique super-elastic properties of Nitinol — critical for implants like stents and heart valves — by preventing recast layers and microcracks.
