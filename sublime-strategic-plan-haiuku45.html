Executive Summary

Company Overview

Sublime Laser, Inc. is a specialized medical device component manufacturer based in Salt Lake City, Utah. The company utilizes advanced femtosecond and fiber laser machining technology to produce precision-engineered tubular components for the neurovascular, cardiovascular, and peripheral vascular medical device industries.

Core Strengths

  • Proprietary femtosecond laser technology
  • Exceptional turnaround times (days vs. weeks)
  • ISO 13485 Medical Device Certification
  • Experienced R&D-focused team
  • Minimal heat-affected zones in machining

Key Market Position

  • Niche market leader in fast-turnaround laser machining
  • Serves primarily R&D and prototyping phases
  • Limited geographic reach (primarily regional)
  • Small, agile team of 4 technical staff
  • Minimal marketing and sales infrastructure
Strategic Vision: Transform from a regional service provider into a national leader in advanced laser machining while expanding into adjacent markets and innovative service offerings.

Core Capabilities & Constraints

Technical Capabilities

  • Femtosecond Lasers: 12-30 micron precision, 0.008"-0.313" OD
  • Fiber Lasers: 20-40 micron kerf, 0.008"-0.500" OD
  • Materials: Stainless steel, Nitinol, precious metals, ceramics, glass
  • Wall Thickness: 0.0005" - 0.040"
  • Tube Length: Up to 120"

Operational Constraints

  • Limited to tubular geometries
  • Small team limits simultaneous projects
  • Minimal marketing/sales infrastructure
  • Single facility location
  • Limited equipment redundancy
  • Geographic reach limited to regional customers

Innovation Area 2: Recurring Revenue Services

1. "Parts on Demand" Subscription

Model: Monthly subscription for component allotments

Structure:

  • Starter: 50 parts/month - $2,500
  • Professional: 200 parts/month - $8,500
  • Enterprise: Unlimited - $25,000+

Value Prop: Predictable budgeting, guaranteed turnaround, priority access

Target Customers: Established device companies with steady prototyping needs

Revenue Potential: $500K-1.5M annually (100-150 subscribers)

2. Design Optimization Consulting

Service: Expert consultation on manufacturability and design

Offerings:

  • Design review workshops: $5K-15K
  • Process optimization: $3K-10K
  • Training programs: $10K-50K
  • Retainer arrangements: $2K-5K/month

Target Customers: Startups, internal R&D teams, contract manufacturers

Revenue Potential: $300K-800K annually

3. White-Label Manufacturing

Model: Provide manufacturing capacity under partner brand

Target Partners:

  • Contract manufacturers needing specialized capability
  • Medical device companies without internal capacity
  • Engineering firms needing manufacturing capability

Structure: Volume-based pricing + service fees (10-30% margin)

Revenue Potential: $1-3M annually (3-5 partnerships)

4. Equipment-as-a-Service (EaaS)

Model: Monthly rental of equipment + technician time

Variants:

  • 4-hour sessions: $2K-3K
  • Day rental: $5K-7K
  • Weekly package: $12K-15K
  • Monthly dedicated: $25K-35K

Target Customers: Customers with intermittent needs

Revenue Potential: $200K-600K annually
