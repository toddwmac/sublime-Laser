Sublime Laser, Inc.

Executive Summary

Sublime Laser, Inc. is an ISO 13485 certified medical device laser machining company specializing in ultra‑precision tubular component manufacturing for neurovascular, cardiovascular, and peripheral vascular device applications.

The company provides rapid-turnaround, tight-tolerance laser machining using femtosecond and fiber laser systems — serving medical device R&D teams that require speed, precision, and manufacturability expertise.

Investment Thesis
  • High-margin precision medical device manufacturing niche
  • ISO 13485 certified (quality system barrier to entry)
  • Deep specialization in nitinol and stainless tubular components
  • Rapid R&D turnaround model creates sticky engineering relationships
  • Strategically positioned as a scalable micro-CDMO platform

Technology & Capabilities

Femtosecond Laser Advantages
  • Virtually zero heat affected zone (HAZ)
  • Micron-level precision (12 micron feature size)
  • Tube diameters: 0.008"–0.313"
  • Wall thickness: 0.0005"–0.013"
  • Materials: Nitinol, stainless steel, precious metals, ceramic, glass
Fiber Laser Capabilities
  • Faster material melt-based cutting
  • Larger diameter tolerance range (up to 0.500")
  • Wall thickness up to 0.040"
  • Feature sizes 20 microns+
Strategic Technical Edge
  • Expertise in nitinol machining (complex, high-skill material)
  • Tube-based geometry specialization
  • 120" max tube length capability
  • Design-to-manufacture collaboration model

Market & Industry Analysis

Industry Position

Sublime operates within the precision medical device contract manufacturing market — specifically laser micromachining for minimally invasive vascular devices.

Market Drivers
  • Growth in minimally invasive surgical devices
  • Increasing demand for neurovascular interventions
  • Cardiovascular disease prevalence growth
  • Expansion of catheter-based technologies

Secular Tailwinds
