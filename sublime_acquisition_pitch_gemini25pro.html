Proprietary process and dual-laser capability

Femtosecond Laser

For ultimate precision and sensitive materials. Vaporizes material with virtually no heat transfer.

  • Laser TypeFemtosecond
  • MaterialsNitinol, Ceramic, Glass, Metals
  • Feature Size≥ 12 microns
  • Beam Kerf12-30 microns
  • Tube Diameter0.008" - 0.313"
Key Differentiator: Minimal to no Heat Affected Zone (HAZ). This preserves the unique properties of materials like Nitinol, a critical requirement for next-gen medical implants.

Fiber Laser

For speed and efficiency in less sensitive applications. Melts material for rapid, accurate production.

  • Laser TypeFiber
  • MaterialsStainless Steel, Precious Metals
  • Feature Size≥ 20 microns

  • Tube Diameter0.008" - 0.500"
Strategic Asset: Offers a cost-effective, high-speed solution for a broad range of standard components, complementing the high-precision femtosecond capability.
